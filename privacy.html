ColorForge — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Our Commitment

ColorForge is built with privacy as a core principle. We collect no personal data, use no analytics, and serve no third-party advertisements or trackers.

Information We Collect

We collect nothing. ColorForge does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Your coloring pages, preferences, and generation history are stored exclusively on your device using local storage. We have no servers that receive or store your data.

AI Generation

When you use the AI coloring page generation feature, your text prompts are sent to Replicate's API for processing. Replicate does not store your prompts or generated images beyond the time needed to complete the generation. No personal identifiers are attached to these requests.

Children's Privacy

ColorForge includes a Kids Mode that complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). In Kids Mode:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No AI text input is available (pre-generated packs only)
  • No social or community features are accessible
  • No external links or advertisements exist
  • All data remains on-device
  • A parental gate (math problem) protects settings access

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed exclusively through Google Play Billing. We never see or store your payment information. Google handles all payment processing according to their privacy policy.

Your Data Rights

Since all your data lives on your device, you control it completely. Clear your app data or uninstall the app to remove everything. No requests to us are necessary.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at: privacy@colorforge.app

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll notify you within the app. Continued use means you accept the updated policy.
